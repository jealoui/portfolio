Jean Delano

I’m a graphic designer with 15+ years of experience turning ideas into visuals that stand out. I started out in editorial design at publishing houses in NYC (remember YM magazine!) and Boston, then shifted into ad agency and in-house roles in Rhode Island. Over the years, I’ve worked with clients in industries like healthcare, toy market, health & wellness, finance, and energy industry.

I’ve got a BFA in Advertising Design from Pratt, and I bring a concept-first mindset to every project. Along the way, my work’s picked up a few honors, including awards from GCI, NEDMA, and the American Inhouse Design Awards.
